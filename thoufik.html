Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone

Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone is the first book in the Harry Potter series written by J.K. Rowling. The story follows a young boy named Harry Potter who discovers that he is a wizard on his eleventh birthday. He is invited to attend Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, where he uncovers the truth about his parents' deaths and his connection to the dark wizard Voldemort.
